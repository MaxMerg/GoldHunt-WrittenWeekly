Look for the gold nugget that you lost! Control the square with the arrow keys. Hit the space bar to do a search.
If the square turns purple you are very far away from your gold nugget. Blue is a little closer, Teal is a good distance away.
Yellow you are very close to your gold. Orange means you are so very close to winning and, Red means you've got your gold.
You can only do 7 searches so make sure to spare them.
